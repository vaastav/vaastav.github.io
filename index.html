My Story

I am currently in my 2nd year of B.Sc. with a major in Computer Science at the University of British Columbia, Vancouver. I have been programming since 6th grade and my love has grown for computer science with each passing day.
My initial attraction to programming can be classified as a direct effect of my avid gaming. My search for answers for how games work, how someone can make their own game and how to solve puzzles landed me into the world of programming. BASIC was the first challenge I encountered on my quest of gaining knowledge to make my own game. Although, I did end up making a game in BASIC, that wasn't what fascinated me the most. What fascinated me the most was the super cool amazing things I could do with programming. This is when I decided to dedicate my whole life to learning all there is about computer science to learn. And I haven't looked back ever since.
